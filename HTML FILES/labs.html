Asana Labs

Explore a variety of yoga poses with detailed descriptions and benefits to enhance your practice.

Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

A foundational pose that stretches the entire body, improves circulation, and calms the mind.

  • Stretches hamstrings and calves
  • Strengthens arms and shoulders
  • Improves digestion
  • Relieves stress and anxiety

Tree Pose (Vrikshasana)

A balancing pose that enhances focus, stability, and concentration.

  • Improves balance and stability in legs
  • Strengthens thighs, calves, ankles, and spine
  • Stretches groins and inner thighs
  • Helps improve concentration

Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II)

An empowering pose that builds strength and stamina in the legs and core.

  • Strengthens legs and ankles
  • Stretches hips, groins, and shoulders
  • Increases stamina and endurance
  • Improves focus and stability

Child's Pose (Balasana)

A restful pose that gently stretches the back, hips, thighs, and ankles while reducing stress and fatigue.

  • Gently stretches hips, thighs, and ankles
  • Relieves stress and fatigue
  • Calms the mind
  • Helps alleviate back and neck pain

Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)

A backbend that stretches the chest, shoulders, and abdomen while strengthening the spine.

  • Strengthens spine
  • Stretches chest, shoulders, and abdomen
  • Helps relieve stress and fatigue
  • Improves flexibility

Triangle Pose (Trikonasana)

A standing pose that stretches the legs, hips, and spine while improving balance and stability.

  • Stretches legs, hips, and spine
  • Strengthens legs, knees, and ankles
  • Improves digestion
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
